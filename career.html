Culinary Career

Job Requirements:

  • Past experience as a working chef.
  • Culinary school degree/diploma.
  • Advanced knowledge of the culinary arts.
  • Portfolio of creative, unique dishes.
  • Etc.

Description:

As a chef you will plan menus, ensure that dishes are nutritious and cost-effective, and you will also have to collaborate with a team of kitchen staff to deliver food that meets the highest quality standards. You must also be a good leader with good organazational skills and thrive for perfect sanatation and quality. The average salary might be low, but as you gain experience, you will get paid more and more.

Salary:

The average Chef can make up to 60k a year.
